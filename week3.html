Cloud Removal Environment

I reproduced the DSen2-CR setup (a deep residual neural network + SAR-optical fusion) to remove clouds from Sentinel-2 imagery. The repository below is now part of the research toolbox and its PDF paper is embedded so the methodology stays readable.

DSen2-CR · ameraner/dsen2-cr

Official implementation of the deep residual network that leverages SAR guidance with a cloud-adaptive loss, matching the globally-sampled training strategy described in the paper.

Visit Repository
  • Optical imagery is foundational across EO applications, yet consistent realizations are blocked by cloud cover.
  • DSen2-CR’s deep residual blocks allow reconstructing optical appearance even under optically thick clouds.
  • SAR-optical fusion adds geomorphic cues, while the cloud-adaptive loss keeps as much surface variability as possible.
  • The Big Data era in satellite sensing makes globally sampled deep learning feasible, matching the paper’s training regime.
